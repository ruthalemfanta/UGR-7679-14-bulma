About me

Albert Einstein has had a profound impact on my life, igniting my curiosity and inspiring me to explore the wonders of the universe. His groundbreaking theories and revolutionary ideas have challenged my perception of reality and sparked a lifelong fascination with physics, the cosmos, and tech. He taught me the importance of bridging the gap between complex ideas and practical applications, which has guided me in translating intricate technical concepts into user-friendly solutions. He indeed shaped me as a person.

Imagine a young girl who played games and read blogs about instances of the world stumbling across this quote. It enlightened me that despite the incredible capabilities of computers, they lack the brilliance and creativity inherent in human beings. They reminded me that I possess a unique power—the power to think critically, to solve complex problems, and to envision innovative solutions. They inspired me to chase my intelligence and to recognize that my potential, when combined with the speed and accuracy of technology, can transcend limits and achieve greatness. That's when i fell in love with computers and their enviroment. They became a dear friend to me. Thus started my journey.

Computers are high-speed, accurate, and stupid; humans are incredibly slow, inaccurate, and brilliant; together they are powerful beyond imagination

Let me walk you through my life

Life after 2002,

I offically joined this world

2010

I got my first phone, the TECNO 1901, It was a momentous occasion, not just for me, but also for my friends. We were eager to witness the wonders this technological marvel had in store. I wasted no time in capturing my first photo.

2012

This year marked the beginning of my deep dive into the world of video games. It became a passion that consumed my days and nights as I embraced the challenges of conquering each level. To overcome these virtual obstacles, I found myself delving into the intricacies of computer manipulation. It was during this journey that I discovered a lifelong companion—Computers.

2017

I encountered my first computer freeze, a daunting moment of frustration. Determined, I turned to the Internet for a solution. With diligence and resourcefulness, I discovered a fix and successfully resolved the issue. A surge of pride washed over me as I reveled in my accomplishment.

2020

The infamous COVID-19 pandemic struck, forcing the world into a prolonged lockdown. In those isolated times, it felt like it was just me and my loyal laptop against the world. It became an opportunity to embark on new ventures. I delved into the realm of programming languages and web design, experimenting with different tools and techniques.

2021

I was blessed to be one of the students to join AAU. It opened a door for many learning opportunities and courses.

2023

Fast forward to today I joined the software engineering department of the university and im still current enrolled their pushing forward as a 3rd year student
